PAYMENT PLAN

  • Tuition is due on a monthly basis
  • $173/month for 30 minute lessons once a week
  • $255/month for 45 minute lessons once a week
  • $337/month for 60 minute lessons once a week

Lessons are given at my studio or on FaceTime. I will also travel to the students home, depending on their location, for an additional $50/month which will be added to the base prices above.

Payment is due on the first day of each month.

STUDENT REQUIREMENTS

  • The student must have access to a piano or a suitable keyboard
  • and is expected to practice every day. When a young student is taking piano lessons for the first time, 3 minutes of daily practice per year of age should be sufficient. For example, if the student is 6 years old, 18 minutes of daily practice would be fine to get started. If the student is 10 years old, 30 minutes would be sufficient, etc.

TIME CANCELLATION POLICY

  • Missed lessons, regardless of notice given, will be counted as lessons. NO tuition credit or refund will be given for missed lessons, regardless of the reason (including illness). It's very important to choose a lesson time in which you feel confident. Please understand the tuition you pay reserves an exclusive weekly time in my schedule for your child, whether or not he or she attends the lesson. These lessons are based on weekly regularity and if your child doesn't attend the lesson, I cannot find another student to fill that slot just for that week.
  • If a lesson is cancelled with a 24 (or more) hour notice, a makeup lesson may be scheduled. Makeup lessons will be given on FaceTime and are subject to availability. If the lesson is canceled without at least a 24 hour notice, no makeup lesson will be offered.
  • If I have to miss a lesson, a makeup lesson will be offered. If no suitable time can be arranged for the makeup lesson, then, and only then, a credit will be applied toward the next month's lessons.

LOCATION

  • Lessons are given at the student's home or my home in Studio City.
